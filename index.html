About Me

Hi, I'm Roli Meggison

As a Licensed Financial Advisor and Coach based in Canada, I'm passionate about making financial planning accessible, understandable, and effective for every Canadian — regardless of where you're starting from.

My approach is personal. I take the time to understand your unique goals, your lifestyle, and your dreams — then build a strategy that actually fits your life. No cookie-cutter plans. No confusing jargon. Just clear, honest guidance you can act on.

Whether you're just starting to invest, planning for retirement, or protecting what you've already built, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.
